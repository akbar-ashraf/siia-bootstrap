SAR 150

Essentials of Internal Auditing- CIA1

7 lectures, 3 quizzes and 3 assessments are deliverable.

Starts: 12 April, Duration: 3 Months, Registrations: Open

Introduction: Part 2 focuses on the practical execution of internal audit engagements. It covers planning, fieldwork, supervision, evidence gathering, evaluation, and communication of results. Additionally, this section delves into the importance of risk assessment in the audit process, ensuring that auditors can identify and prioritize areas of concern. It also emphasizes the need for effective teamwork and collaboration among audit staff to enhance the quality of findings. Furthermore, we will explore best practices for documenting audit procedures and maintaining transparency throughout the engagement, which are crucial for building trust with stakeholders.
